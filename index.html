Introduction

Welcome to my webpage! I am currently working on my Ph.D in Computer Science at the University of Central Florida. I joined the Department of Computer Science in Fall 2018. My primary advisor is Dr. Xinwen Fu, and my co-advisor is Dr. Cliff Zou. I am primarily interested in IoT security and forensics.

Prior to UCF, I received my Bachelor of Science from Stetson University in May 2018. My advisor was Dr. Daniel Plante.

You can check out some of my current and former research projects, my CV, and contact information through this webpage.
